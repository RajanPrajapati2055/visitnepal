RARA LAKE: PEACE AND BEAUTY AT A PLACE

Rara lake is the biggest and deepest lake of Nepal located in Jumla and Mugu districts which lie at an altitude of 2990 meter and covers an area of 10.8 square kilometers. Rara lake is the main feature of the Rara National Park.Rara Lake has much unique flora and fauna importance of rare and vulnerable species. The lake is popular destination with a rough route in western Nepal for trekkers.
