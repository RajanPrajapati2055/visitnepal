GOSAIKUNDA: AWESOME TREK TO GO FOR

Gosaikunda Lake is one of the most famous religious and pilgrimage sites of Nepal. It is located at an altitude of 4,380 meter and surrounded by peaks in Langtang National Park in Rasuwa district with a surface of 13.8 ha(34 acres). It is surrounded by the mountain ranges,amazing scenario, and delightful forest. Every year in Janai Purnima,August full moon day thousands of pilgrims visit Gosaikunda, take a bath in the lake amd worship lord Shiva. Gosaikunda is one of the best places for trekking destinations.
